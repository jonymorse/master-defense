Instructions:

1. Click "Enable Gesture Control" to activate webcam hand tracking

2. Move your hand from the left half to the right half to go to the next slide

3. Move your hand from the right half to the left half to go to the previous slide

4. Watch for the center line in the webcam view - just cross it with your hand

5. No need for fast movements - simply cross from one side to the other
